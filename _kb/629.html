Question

How do I delete a Docker Hub or Docker Cloud account?

Answer

Deleting your Docker Hub/Cloud account will permanently remove your account and cannot be recovered. Your Docker Hub/Cloud account may also be linked to other Docker products and services, so deleting your account will also disable access to those products and services.

Before submitting a request to delete your account, please complete the following:

  • Delete all public and private repositories for this account.
  • If you have a subscription plan, cancel any active subscriptions on Docker Hub and Docker Cloud and confirm that your account has been downgraded to the free plan.
  • If you belong to an organization, remove your account from all organizations. If you are the sole owner of any organization, either add someone to the " owners " team and then remove yourself from the organization or submit a request to delete the organization as well.
  • If you have a license key you wish to keep, download the key from your Docker Hub account. The download link will no longer be available after the account deletion.
  • Unlink your GitHub and Bitbucket source providers on Docker Hub here and Docker Cloud here .
  • Unlink all public cloud providers on Docker Cloud.
  • Delete all nodes, services, and stacks on Docker Cloud.

Once you've completed the steps above, please open a Customer Service Request using the email address associated with the account and provide the Docker ID of the account you wish to delete.
